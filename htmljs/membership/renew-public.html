Renew your Membership

Our payment processes have changed and for your convenience, as of 1 July 2020 membership is now offered as an automatic renewal via an annual or monthly credit card payment.

To renew your membership online, please follow these steps:

  • Click on myACHPER to login with your username and password
  • Once logged in, click myACHPER to enter your account
  • Click Renew Now and follow the prompts to set up your payment

Members who are unable to complete online payment should complete the Membership Form and payment will be processed as soon as possible.

Need more information? Please don't hesitate to contact us.
